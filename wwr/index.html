Visit our Trips page for an adventure of a lifetime!

Rafting and whitewater rafting are recreational outdoor activities which use an inflatable raft to navigate a river or other body of water. This is often done on whitewater or different degrees of rough water. Dealing with risk is often a part of the experience.

No matter what part of the adventure you're on, there is always something you can learn from it. The people, the animals, the plants, the scenery and even the river itself. There are even legends, tales and the famed “ghost stories” to learn as well.
